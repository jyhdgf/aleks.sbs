How ALEKS Works

Our platform uses adaptive technology to identify what you know and what you're ready to learn next.

1

Initial Knowledge Check

ALEKS begins with an initial knowledge check to accurately determine what a student knows, doesn't know, and is ready to learn next.

2

Personalized Learning Path

Based on the assessment results, ALEKS creates a personalized learning path with the topics the student is ready to learn.

3

Mastery-Based Learning

Students work through their learning path, mastering each topic through practice problems and explanations.

4

Continuous Assessment

ALEKS periodically reassesses students to ensure retention and to update their knowledge state.
